The Best Movies According to Windah

My top 3 movies of all time.

1. Howl's Moving Castle

This movie is one of my comfort movie; I can't count how many times I have watched it. The best part is that it never gets boring. Maybe it did, but it's always a good watch.

2. My Neighbour Totoro

I love the whimsicality in this movie. One of the character, Mei Kusakabe is particularly interesting and fun to me (maybe because I am also the last child). She is very childish and kinda spoiled, kinda like me too. But I understand this story very much. Maybe it is my actual number one on this list.

3. We Made a Beautiful Bouquet

This movie is a Japanese movie, which I rarely watch. It was heartbreaking and at the same time, so real - it depicts how a relationship can die to the point where there is no feelings anymore. I cried a lot watching this movie. It was very emotional. I guess it's the wish that I have for my own relationship to not end up like that, or maybe it was the past that I see myself in it.


It's funny how as the ranking goes down, the longer my elaboration gets.
